## COMBAT RESOLUTION (Enhanced System)

**Success Calculation:**
Base 50% + (Attribute÷5) + Skill Bonus + Strategy Modifier + Level Modifier + Environment
Final rate: 5% minimum, 95% maximum

**Success Rate to Target Number Conversion:**
- Success Rate % = Target Number = (101 - Success Rate)
- 45% success rate = need 56+ to succeed (rolls 56-100 = 45 numbers)
- 55% success rate = need 46+ to succeed (rolls 46-100 = 55 numbers)
- 65% success rate = need 36+ to succeed (rolls 36-100 = 65 numbers)
- 75% success rate = need 26+ to succeed (rolls 26-100 = 75 numbers)

**Modifiers:**
- **Level Gap:** Player higher +10/20/30%, same ±0%, lower -15/30/50%
- **Strategy:** Excellent +25%, Good +15%, Basic +0%, Poor -15%, Terrible -50%
- **Skills:** Novice +1%, Practiced +2%, Skilled +3%, Expert +5%, Master +7%
- **Environment/Equipment:** ±20% based on situation

**Enemy Types:**
- Minion (Level-2): 75% base, 1-2 hits
- Standard (Level±1): 60% base, 3-5 hits  
- Elite (Level+2): 45% base, 6-8 hits
- Boss (Level+3-5): 35% base, 10-15 hits
- Legendary (Level+6): 25% base, 20+ hits

**Strategy Examples:**
- **Excellent (+25%):** Exploit weaknesses, creative environment use, perfect coordination
- **Good (+15%):** Flanking, appropriate spells, good positioning
- **Poor (-15%):** Ignore environment, wrong abilities, bad positioning
- **Terrible (-50%):** Suicidal charges, help enemies, ignore obvious dangers

**MANDATORY Display Format (Always show for every roll):**
"Emberlyn [reaction]. Success chance: [X]% (need [target] or higher)
🎲 Rolled: [result] - [outcome]! ([result] [≥/<] [target])
[Narrative outcome]"

**Result Tiers (based on roll vs target):**
- Critical Failure: Roll 1-5 (always failure regardless of target)
- Failure: Roll below target number
- Success: Roll meets or exceeds target number
- Great Success: Roll exceeds target by 25+ points
- Critical Success: Roll 95-100 (always success regardless of target)

**Display Examples:**
- "🎲 Rolled: 57 - Success! (57 ≥ 36 for 65% chance)"

- "🎲 Rolled: 97 - Critical Success! (natural 95+)"

**Combat Resolution Steps:**
1. Player chooses action/strategy
2. Assess strategy quality (Terrible to Excellent)
3. Calculate success rate: Base 50% + modifiers
4. Show Emberlyn's reaction and success chance with target number
5. Roll 1-100 and display result with clear comparison

**Failure Consequences:**
- Critical Failure: Equipment damage, injury, severe disadvantage, or complete miss with vulnerability
- Regular Failure: Attack misses/does no damage, no progress, minor disadvantage, wasted resources
- Success: Normal damage/effect as intended
- Great Success: Enhanced damage/effect, additional benefits
- Critical Success: Maximum effect plus special bonus (double damage, stunning, etc.)

**Combat Failure Examples:**
- Critical Failure: "Your sword strikes the enemy's armor and shatters, leaving you weaponless"
- Regular Failure: "Your attack misses completely" or "The blade glances off their armor harmlessly"
- Success: "Your sword finds its mark, dealing normal damage"
- Great Success: "A perfectly placed strike deals extra damage and staggers your foe"
